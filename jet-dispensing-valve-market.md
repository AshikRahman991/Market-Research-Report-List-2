<p><ul><li>Contact Jet Valves</li><li>Non-contact Jet Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jet Dispensing Valve market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact jet valves. Contact jet valves operate by making direct contact with the substrate during fluid dispensing, ideal for precise application of glues or adhesives. In contrast, non-contact jet valves dispense fluid without physical contact, utilizing air pressure to create droplets, which allows for applications where cleanliness and minimal material wastage are crucial. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, automotive, and packaging, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in dispensing processes.</p></p>
