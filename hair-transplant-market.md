<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair transplant market encompasses two primary techniques: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of scalp from which individual hair follicles are harvested, which may leave a linear scar. In contrast, FUE extracts individual hair follicles directly from the scalp, minimizing scarring and allowing for faster recovery. Both methods have unique advantages, catering to different patient needs and preferences, driving their respective market growth and adoption in the cosmetic surgery industry.</p></p>
