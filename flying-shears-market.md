<p><ul><li>Mobile Flying Shear</li><li>Drum Flying Shear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flying shears market is divided into two main types: mobile flying shears and drum flying shears. Mobile flying shears are versatile, allowing for quick adjustments and can operate across various production lines, making them ideal for dynamic manufacturing environments. In contrast, drum flying shears are designed for high-speed applications, typically used in continuous production processes where precision and efficiency are critical. Both types enhance productivity by enabling real-time cutting of materials as they move through the production line.</p></p>
