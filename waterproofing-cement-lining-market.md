<p><p>The waterproofing cement lining market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component waterproofing cement is pre-mixed and ready for use, simplifying application and ensuring consistency in performance. In contrast, two-component systems require mixing of a resin and hardener, offering greater flexibility and enhanced waterproofing properties. These systems are utilized in various applications, including construction, infrastructure, and wastewater management, where durability and resistance to water infiltration are essential for long-lasting results.</p></p>
