<p><p>The energy-efficient glass market is primarily segmented into hard coat and soft coat types. Hard coat glass features a durable, transparent layer that reflects infrared light, enhancing thermal insulation while providing a robust surface. This type is suitable for various applications due to its strength. In contrast, soft coat glass has a thin, reflective layer applied in a vacuum process, offering superior thermal performance and better light transmission. It’s ideal for residential and commercial buildings requiring optimal energy conservation.</p></p>
