<p><p>The Tubal Rings and Clips market encompasses two primary types of contraceptive methods for female sterilization. Tubal Rings are silicone devices placed in the fallopian tubes to block sperm from reaching the eggs, offering a permanent solution to prevent pregnancy. Tubal Clips, on the other hand, are metal or plastic clamps applied to the fallopian tubes, effectively sealing them to achieve the same goal. Both methods are effective, making them popular choices for women seeking permanent contraception options.</p></p>
