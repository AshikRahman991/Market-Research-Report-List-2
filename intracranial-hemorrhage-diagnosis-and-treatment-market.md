<p><p>Intracranial hemorrhage (ICH) refers to bleeding within the skull, which can lead to serious complications. Additionally, the rise in traumatic brain injuries and vascular disorders contributes significantly to market expansion. Latest trends indicate a shift towards minimally invasive procedures and enhanced imaging techniques, thereby improving patient outcomes and reducing recovery times.
