<p><p>The asthma management products market includes three primary types: corticosteroids, bronchodilators, and others. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory medications that reduce airway swelling and help prevent asthma attacks. Bronchodilators are quick-relief medications that relax bronchial muscles, providing immediate relief of asthma symptoms. The "others" category encompasses alternative treatments, combination therapies, and innovative delivery devices. Together, these products play a crucial role in controlling asthma symptoms, improving patients' quality of life, and reducing the frequency of exacerbations.</p></p>
