<p><ul><li>Strand Palletizing System</li><li>Underwater Palletizing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pelletizer market consists of two primary types: Strand Palletizing System and Underwater Palletizing System. The Strand Palletizing System involves forming plastic strands that are cooled and cut into pellets, ideal for materials requiring surface cooling. In contrast, the Underwater Palletizing System involves cutting the molten plastic underwater, which allows for immediate cooling and minimizes oxidation, resulting in cleaner pellets. Both systems cater to different production needs and material characteristics within the plastics manufacturing industry.</p></p>
