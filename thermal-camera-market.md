<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal camera market is categorized into short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave length cameras based on their sensor sensitivity to different infrared wavelengths. Short-wave length cameras detect radiation from 0.9 to 3 micrometers, ideal for high-temperature applications. Mid-wave length cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are widely used in military and surveillance. Long-wave length cameras, spanning 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in detecting heat emitted by objects, making them suitable for building inspections and environmental monitoring. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
