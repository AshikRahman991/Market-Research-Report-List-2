<p><ul><li>High Temperature Sterilization</li><li>Low Temperature Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization equipment market is categorized into high-temperature and low-temperature sterilization types. High-temperature sterilization typically utilizes steam or dry heat methods, effectively eliminating microorganisms and spores, making it ideal for heat-resistant medical instruments. In contrast, low-temperature sterilization employs processes like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide vapor, suitable for delicate equipment that cannot withstand high heat. Both methods cater to different healthcare needs, ensuring sterility and safety in medical environments while addressing varying material compatibility and effectiveness requirements.</p></p>
