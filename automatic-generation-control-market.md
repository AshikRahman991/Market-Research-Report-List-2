<p><strong>The Automatic Generation Control Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Turbine-Governor Control (TGC)</li><li>Load-Frequency Control (LFC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Generation Control (AGC) involves managing electricity generation to maintain system frequency and reliability. In the Turbine-Governor Control (TGC) market, individual power plants adjust their output based on frequency deviations, allowing for rapid response to disturbances. Conversely, Load-Frequency Control (LFC) functions on a larger scale, coordinating multiple generators to balance load and frequency over longer periods. Together, these market types ensure that supply matches demand, enhancing grid stability and efficiency in real-time operations.</p></p>
