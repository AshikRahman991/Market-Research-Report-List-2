<p><ul><li>Self-operate Thermostatic Radiator Valves</li><li>Electronic Thermostatic Radiator Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-regulating thermostatic radiator valves (TRVs) market is divided into two main types: self-operating and electronic TRVs. Self-operating TRVs function mechanically, using wax or liquid-filled sensors to adjust the flow of hot water based on room temperature, enabling energy-efficient heating without external power. In contrast, electronic TRVs utilize electronic sensors and programmable settings to optimize heating schedules, allowing for precise temperature control and remote accessibility. Both types contribute to improved energy management and enhanced comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
