<p><p>The armored cable market features two primary types: Metal Tape Armored Cable and Metal Wire Armored Cable. Metal Tape Armored Cable utilizes a protective metal tape layer, providing lightweight, flexible protection against mechanical damage and moisture. In contrast, Metal Wire Armored Cable comprises a robust wire mesh, offering higher durability and strength for heavy-duty applications. Both types are widely used in industrial and commercial settings where enhanced protection for electrical conductors is essential, ensuring safety and reliability in challenging environments.</p></p>
