<p><ul><li>Telescopic Suspension</li><li>Telescopic Suspension (upside down)</li><li>Spring Loaded Hydraulic Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorcycle suspension systems are essential for managing ride quality and handling. Telescopic suspension is the most common type, using a pair of fork tubes to absorb shocks. Upside-down telescopic suspension, or inverted forks, enhances rigidity and improves handling by positioning the larger diameter tube at the bottom. Spring-loaded hydraulic suspension combines springs and hydraulic dampers to offer adjustable shock absorption, providing a smoother ride and better control over varied terrains. Each type contributes uniquely to performance and comfort.</p></p>
