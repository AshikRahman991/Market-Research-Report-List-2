<p><p>The Contrast Injector Market is divided into two main types: single-head and dual-head contrast injectors. Single-head contrast injectors are designed to administer contrast agents from a single source, ideal for basic imaging procedures. In contrast, dual-head contrast injectors can deliver two different contrast agents simultaneously, enhancing the versatility and efficiency of imaging operations. Both types are essential in medical imaging, particularly in enhancing the quality of procedures like CT and MRIs, improving diagnostic outcomes.</p></p>
