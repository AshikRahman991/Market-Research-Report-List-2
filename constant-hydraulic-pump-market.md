<p><p>The Constant Hydraulic Pump market is primarily divided into two types: axial pumps and radial pumps. Axial pumps operate by drawing fluid parallel to the pump shaft, characterized by high efficiency and flow rates, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent fluid movement. Conversely, radial pumps move fluid perpendicular to the shaft, providing high pressure and compact designs. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including construction, manufacturing, and agricultural sectors, ensuring efficient hydraulic systems tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
