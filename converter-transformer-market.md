<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Back-To-Back</li><li>Bipolar</li><li>Multi-Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Converter Transformer market is segmented into four main types: Monopolar, Back-to-Back, Bipolar, and Multi-Terminal. Monopolar transformers facilitate single-direction power flow, ideal for long-distance transmission. Back-to-Back transformers connect two AC networks, enabling efficient power exchange. Bipolar transformers support two-way power flow, enhancing transmission efficiency and reliability. Multi-Terminal transformers connect multiple stations, allowing complex grid structures and improving stability. Each type plays a crucial role in the evolving landscape of power transmission and integration of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
