<p><ul><li>Sprinkler</li><li>Drip</li><li>Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic irrigation system market encompasses three main types: sprinkler, drip, and surface irrigation. Sprinkler systems distribute water through overhead pipes, simulating natural rainfall, making them suitable for various terrains. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to the plant roots via a network of tubes, minimizing wastage and enhancing efficiency. Surface irrigation relies on gravitational flow to distribute water across the field, suitable for flat landscapes. Each system offers distinct advantages, catering to different agricultural needs and water conservation strategies.</p></p>
