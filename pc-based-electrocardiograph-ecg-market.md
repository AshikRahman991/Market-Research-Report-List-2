<p><p>The PC-based electrocardiograph (ECG) market is segmented into two main types: standalone and cloud-based systems. Standalone ECG systems operate independently on a local computer, providing reliable data processing and analysis without internet dependency, making them suitable for hospitals and clinics with limited connectivity. In contrast, cloud-based ECG systems leverage internet connectivity to store, share, and analyze ECG data remotely, facilitating collaboration among healthcare professionals and enhancing accessibility for patients, ultimately improving diagnostic capabilities and patient care.</p></p>
