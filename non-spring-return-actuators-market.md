<p><ul><li>Floating Control</li><li>Proportional Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Spring Return Actuators are classified primarily into two market types: Floating Control and Proportional Control. Floating Control actuators operate based on on/off positions, allowing for simple implementation in systems requiring straightforward valve operation. In contrast, Proportional Control actuators provide variable control, enabling precise adjustment of valve positions based on flow or pressure changes. This distinction allows industries to choose the actuator type that best fits their operational requirements, balancing cost and performance in automation applications.</p></p>
