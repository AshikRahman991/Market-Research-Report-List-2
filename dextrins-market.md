<p><p>The dextrins market is primarily segmented into maltodextrin and cyclodextrin types. Maltodextrin, a white powder derived from starch, is widely used in the food and beverage industry as a thickener, sweetener, or filler, enhancing texture and consistency. Cyclodextrin, derived from starch hydrolysis, has a unique ring structure that forms inclusion complexes with various substances, finding applications in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food preservation. Both types cater to growing demands for multifunctional ingredients in diverse sectors, driving market growth.</p></p>
