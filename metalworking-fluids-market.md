<p><p>The metalworking fluids market encompasses four key types: </p><p>1. **Metal Removal Fluids** are used in processes like cutting and grinding, enhancing tool performance and surface finish. </p><p>2. **Metal Forming Fluids** aid in shaping metals through processes like stamping and drawing, providing lubrication and cooling. </p><p>3. **Metal Protecting Fluids** prevent corrosion and oxidation on metal surfaces during storage and manufacturing. </p><p>4. **Metal Treating Fluids** facilitate heat treatment processes, improving the properties of metals through chemical and thermal treatments. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing metalworking efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
