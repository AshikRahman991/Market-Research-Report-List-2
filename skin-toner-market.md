<p><p>The skin toner market comprises three main types: Skin Bracers or Fresheners, Skin Tonics, and Astringents. Skin Bracers are lightweight, hydrating formulas that soothe and refresh the skin without stripping moisture. Skin Tonics provide mild exfoliation and improved skin texture, often enriched with herbal extracts. Astringents, typically containing higher alcohol content, are designed to cleanse and tighten pores, making them suitable for oily or acne-prone skin. Together, these products cater to diverse skincare needs and preferences, enhancing overall skin health.</p></p>
